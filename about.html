About Us

Your trusted sourcing partner from China

Who We Are

Tuochen Trade (抚州市临川区拓辰贸易商行) is a professional sourcing and export company based in Fuzhou, China. We specialize in connecting global distributors and wholesalers with quality Chinese manufacturers.

Our main focus is on packaging equipment, including labeling machines, filling machines, and packaging machinery. We work directly with certified factories in Guangzhou and surrounding areas to ensure competitive prices and reliable quality.

Our Mission

To help international buyers source quality products from China with transparency, competitive pricing, and excellent service. We believe in building long-term partnerships based on trust and mutual benefit.

Why Work With Us?

  • Direct Factory Connections: We work directly with manufacturers, cutting out middlemen.
  • Quality Assurance: All products are inspected before shipping.
  • Competitive Pricing: Factory-direct prices with flexible payment terms.
  • Full Export Support: We handle documentation, shipping, and customs clearance assistance.
  • After-Sales Service: Technical support and spare parts supply available.
